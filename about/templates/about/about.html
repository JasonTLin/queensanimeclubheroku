About Us




Description

Queen's Anime Club is the largest anime communities located in Kingston, Ontario. We are a student-run club under AMS focused towards "providing an inclusive environment for individuals within the anime and its related communities". Although "anime" is in our name, many members also enjoy manga, video games, board games and card games.


Events

Anime Screenings- We hold anime screenings bi-weekly every Friday at Dupuis 215. They will start 6:00pm and go until 9:00-9:30pm. We will watch seasonal animes however under special occasions we may decide to watch animes of a certain "theme". Feel free to send any recommendations. Snacks and refreshments will be provided.

Social Events- Sushi Nights, hot pot, cosplay, video/board games night and much more. We are also open to recommendations you may have!


Membership

Membership is $10 CAD per semester. With it comes a plethora of privileges including: unrestricted access to our anime screenings, special social events and you get a unique membership card. The membership card is required to receive benefits such as discounts from our sponsors. It will also be necessary if you wish to run for an executive position.
